About EventKonnect

EventKonnect is a smart, web-based platform designed specifically for academic institutions and community organizations. Our goal is to digitize the fragmented event management process, moving away from manual spreadsheets and paper registration.

Built with a focus on non-commercial use, we prioritize accessibility, role-based control, and meaningful post-event engagement.

QR Ticketing

Server-side validated entries for secure campus access.

College Mode

Restricted event access with manual student verification.

AI Chatbot

24/7 guidance for event discovery and registration help.

Memory Book

Preserving event highlights through photos and attendee notes.
